Bachelor In Sharia And Modern Law

Details

This is a two-year course in Islamic studies and Modern Law. The student who enrolled in this program would get advanced training in the fundamental Islamic principles including textual analysis, research methodology in classical Islamic scholarship and practical interpretation of the text. The students who get enrolled in this course will attain a Bachelor in law and business administration from Markaz Law College along with the Master degree in Islamic studies.

Eligibility
  • • Bachelor in Islamic studies approved by Jamiath-ul-hind al Islamiya or equivalent course.
  • • Bachelor education or equivalent course in modern science.

Masters In Sharia And Mdern Law

Details

This is a five year course with particular focus in the comparative Islamic law and modern law. While conferring a bachelor in law, this course ensures a bachelor degree in Sharia studies too, which follows the methods and syllabus of traditional/classical Islamic leaning. This double degree will benefit the students in order to open new research avenues in the law scholarship. The students who get enrolled in this course will attain a Bachelor in law and business administration from Markaz Law College along with the Bachelor degree in Islamic studies

Eligibility
  • • Higher secondary education or equivalent course in modern science.
  • • Higher secondary in Islamic studies or equivalent course.

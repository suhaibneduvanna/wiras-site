INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON PLURALISM AND HUMAN RIGHTS IN ISLAM

On 02-03 February 2021

Get Started! Register

WORLD INSTITUTE FOR RESEARCH IN ADVANCED SCIENCES

WORLD INSTITUTE FOR RESEARCH AND ADVANCED SCIENCES (WIRAS) , help you pursue advanced learning in contemporary sciences which will , in turn, enable our students to communicate with contemporary society ,being aware of its pulse and flaws.

In a world where modern science is considered a tool to profess liberalism with morality as a mere discipline ,WIRAS pioneers a world-class , research-oriented educational system equally blended with morality based discourses to create a pool of intellectuals who are academically sound and socially committed. Along with providing an array of disciplines varying from modern sciences to theology and modern law, WIRAS promotes tolerance ,harmony and respect towards all the fellow beings which have made us reserve a brand of our own among other institutions of our kind.

ICPHRI bring together leading academic scholars and researchers to share their research results on all aspects of Islam, Pluralism and human rights. It also provides a premier interdisciplinary platform to discuss about the misunderstandings of the academic world about Islam and the dignity ensures to humanity and discuss various issues, concepts and concerns over Islam and Muslim society.The present conference focuses on a specific theme Pluralism and Human rights in Islam which is very relevant in the arena of Islamic social and intellectual gamut. Islam introduced new principles of social life into the world. It was indeed to respect the rights of others and live peaceably with the concern of humanity and dignity. Pluralism and human rights were introduced by Islam in concerning the each individual as a member of the society and ensured private and public rights. Whereas the west is involved merely only in documents or paper to keep humanity and let to have rights to people, even when the west have been involved to make human rights after the world wars and continuous struggles of people. When all religions, beliefs and cultures live in harmony, co-existence and tolerance, then religious pluralism can be said to be there. A pluralistic religion accepts people of other religions instead of accepting beliefs of other religions. Islam teaches that the ideas of other religions are great for its followers and should not give way to contempt and criticism. In sense, Islam is the only religion that has given pluralism and ensured human rights to keep the dignity of human being in living in a society, to the world.

THEMES

  1. Extremism in believers, the coalition with Islamic law
  2. Penal codes and rights in Islam, Structuring a humanitarian Society.
  3. War in sematic religion the Cultural history of human rights
  4. The abolition of Race and slavery; Islamic Laws for human rights
  5. Economic rights; the humanitarian views of Islam
  6. The legitimacy of freedom of expression, the logical significance of Islamic law
  7. Democratic pluralism the root of Islam in peace and tolerance
  8. Women and gender, fundamentals of Islam; a negotiation with human rights
  9. Inclusive Islam and the pluralistic rights to non-Muslims
  10. Feminine social aura; the way of Islamic sheltering
  11. Islamic epitome and methods of conflict resolution

GUIDELINES

  • Paper can be written and presented in English/Arabic/ Malayalam/Urdu Languages and submitted through EasyChair
  • Paper must be in Microsoft Word format, double spaced, '12' font size with Times New Roman.
  • The Arabic and Urdu fonts and styles can be arranged accordingly.
  • Full tentative title of the manuscript must be included in the paper.
  • The abstract should be not more than 300 words. Please limit the use of acronyms, abbreviations and references in the abstract.
  • Keywords for the article: 3-5 keywords are sufficient.
  • Referencing should be in standard format. We recommend the Harvard referencing system.
  • Please SUBMIT a copy of the paper and abstract along with the bio data into icphr.academe@gmail.com
  • Along with the submission of full papers, the authors are requested to send their willingness to publish their papers in conference proceedings/ books/ journals (as the organizing committee may decide).
  • Those whose final paper is accepted are only allowed to present the paper in conference.
  • The acceptance of abstract is not a promise to participate in the conference. However, the participants (who are not presenting papers) shall be registered separately icphr.academe@gmail.com to attend in the proceeding of the conference.
  • The presenters are requested to brief the paper in PPT format while presenting in the session as separate attachments on the submission form.
    • Please upload the following:
  • A short biography of each author pointing educational and relevant work experience to use in conference brochures in case the paper is accepted.
  • Good passport-size picture of the authors (not compulsory) to use in conference brochures.

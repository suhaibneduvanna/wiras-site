Applications for WIRAS PG course are invited
Interview : 9:00 AM Tuesday, March 23

Knowledge City: World Institute for Research in Advanced Sciences (WIRAS), Islamic education Institution in Markaz Knowledge City has invited applications for Post Graduation in Islamic Studies & Modern Law.

Apart from PG course of WIRAS, Students who completes the course have also the opportunity to become Advocate / Unani Doctor upto their choice.

Admission to the PG course is open to those who have a degree in Mukhtasar or have completed the fifth year of Jamiatul Hind degree with a bachelor degree recognized by Calicut University.

Date of interview will be informed later

Only those who registered online will have the opportunity to attend the camp.

There will be a written test on the same day too.

Those who pass the exam can only ensure their admission after the final viva interview.

Our Programmes

Bachelor In Sharia And Modern Law

Read more

WIRAS offers a curriculum that provides its students with a foundation in the intellectual heritage of two major laws. Islamic law and Modern Law. This is a five year course with particular focus in the comparative Islamic law and modern law. While conferring a bachelor in law, this course ensures a bachelor degree in Islamic theology, which follows the methods and syllabus of traditional/classical Islamic learning. This dual degree program will benefit the students to open doors of new research avenues in Islamic and modern law scholarship. The students who get enrolled in this course will attain a Bachelor in law and business administration from Markaz Law College along with the Bachelor degree in Islamic studies.

Eligibility
  • • Higher secondary education or equivalent course as provided by government or other government approved agencies.
  • • Higher secondary in Islamic studies or equivalent course from any approved institutions.

Bachelor In Sharia And Medical Science

Read more

This is a five year course with particular focus in the comparative Islamic law along with Bachelors in Unani Medical Science(BUMS). This will create a pool of scholars who will rewind the past of Muslim legacy like Ibnu Sina(Avicenna) and Abubakr razi(Razes) who were both Islamic Scholars and doctors as well.

Eligibility
  • • Higher secondary education or equivalent course in modern science.
  • • Higher secondary in Islamic studies or equivalent course.

Masters In Sharia And Modern Law

Read more

This is a two-year course in Islamic studies and Modern Law. The student who enrolled in this program would get advanced training in the fundamentals of Islamic principles including textual analysis, research methodology of classical Islamic scholarship and practical interpretation of the texts. The students who get enrolled in this course will attain a Bachelors in law and business administration from Markaz Law College along with the Master’s degree in Islamic studies. This is a completely research-oriented course including studies of classical Arabic language and literature; Qur’an and Qur’anic commentaries; Islamic philosophy,Jurisprudence, theology, and mysticism; Islamic law; and the history of Islam.

Eligibility
  • • Bachelors in Islamic studies approved by Jamiath-ul-hind al Islamiya or any equivalent courses from any recognized universities or Jamias.
  • • Bachelors education or equivalent course in modern disciplines from any universities

Applications for WIRAS PG course are invited
Interview : 9:00 AM Tuesday, March 23

Knowledge City: World Institute for Research in Advanced Sciences (WIRAS), Islamic education Institution in Markaz Knowledge City has invited applications for Post Graduation in Islamic Studies & Modern Law.

Apart from PG course of WIRAS, Students who completes the course have also the opportunity to become Advocate / Unani Doctor upto their choice.

Admission to the PG course is open to those who have a degree in Mukhtasar or have completed the fifth year of Jamiatul Hind degree with a bachelor degree recognized by Calicut University.

Date of interview will be informed later

Only those who registered online will have the opportunity to attend the camp.

There will be a written test on the same day too.

Those who pass the exam can only ensure their admission after the final viva interview.
